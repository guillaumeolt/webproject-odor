Overview

odorwebsite is a web server that compiles information about odorous molecules, their odours and their olfactory receptors. The server integrates several tools to analyse the interactions between odour molecules, olfactory receptors and their physico-chemical links. We freely give access to a compile database of 5976 chemicals with 5907 chemicals with known odors among which 284 are odorless. The database contains 231 olfactory receptors for which at least one chemical-or interaction is known. There are 55979 known odorant-odors links and 2070 known odorant-or interactions. The site provides several tools like the possibility to search for information on a compound according to its structure predict the odorant power of a molecule or its potential to interact with olfactory recptors. we facilitate the possibility to apply a docking protocol to the molecules and proteins of the database by redireting to the Seamdock server.
