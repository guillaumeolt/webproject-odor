Overview

Pred-O3 is a web server that compiles information about odorant molecules, their odors and their olfactory receptors (OR). The server integrates several tools to analyse the interactions between odorant, olfactory receptors and their physico-chemical links. We freely give access to a compile database of 5802 chemicals with known odors. The database contains 385 olfactory receptors (from human, mouse and rat species) for which at least one chemical-or interaction is known. There are 36016 known odorant-odors links and 2425 known odorant-or interactions. The website proposes to search for olfactory receptors and/or odor annotated for a chemical (using its name or chemical structure). Also, through several tools, it gives the opportunity to predict olfactory receptors and odors associated for any small molecules using graphical neural network (GNN). Finally, the webserver predicts the ligand-olfactory receptor binding mode for a set of 1529 human OR model.

Tools

Structural similarity

Similar compounds from the database can be searched by 2 methods : The similarity search based on a Tanimoto score The substructure search where a substructure pattern of an odorant molecule can be searched.

Prediction

Predict the potential odors of a molecule or its potential interacting olfactory receptors. Use two prediction models based on a graphical Graphical Neural Network (GNN) developped by Achebouche et al.

Docking

Select a chemical and an olfactory receptor and launch the SeamDock webserver [Murail et al] to predict ligand-OR binding mode.
